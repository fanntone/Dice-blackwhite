How to Play

Dice colors: Red = 1, 4 Black = 2, 3, 5, 6

  1. The starting player looks at their dice, then picks one of six calls:
    Remove RedRemove Black Remove Big (4 5 6)Remove Small (1 2 3) Remove Odd (1 3 5)Remove Even (2 4 6)
  2. After the call, everyone reveals and removes the matching dice(including the caller).
  3. Put remaining dice back, re-roll, and the next player repeats.
  4. When a player runs out of dice → that player loses.
  5. If someone is wiped out on the opening round's first call → One-Hit Kill, double penalty!
  6. If 2+ players lose at once, the last one in order starts the next game.
